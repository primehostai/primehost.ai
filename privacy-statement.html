Privacy Statement

1. Introduction

This Privacy Statement explains how PrimeHost AI collects, uses, and protects personal information gathered through our services.

2. Information Collection

Types of information collected:

  • User-provided information (e.g., name, contact details) voluntarily shared while using with our services.

3. Use of Information

The collected information is used for:

  • Facilitating guest communication and providing assistance through our services.
  • Improving the functionality and user experience.
  • Analytics and research to enhance services.

4. Data Sharing

PrimeHost AI does not sell or rent personal information to third parties. Information may be shared with trusted service providers or partners involved in delivering the services.

We employ industry-standard security measures to safeguard user data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure. Users share information at their own risk.

6. Data Retention

Personal information collected is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Statement.

7. User Rights

Users have the right to access, update, or request deletion of their personal information. Opt-out mechanisms for receiving communications or withdrawing consent are available.

8. Third-Party Links

Our services might contain links to third-party websites or services. This Privacy Statement applies solely to information collected our services.

9. Compliance with Laws

PrimeHost AI complies with relevant data protection laws and regulations applicable to the collection and processing of personal information.

10. Updates to Privacy Statement

This Privacy Statement may be updated periodically. Users will be notified of any changes.

11. Contact Information

For inquiries or concerns regarding privacy practices, please contact at info@primehost.ai.
