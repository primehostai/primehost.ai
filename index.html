What we do

PrimeHost automates the communication between properties and their guests, utilizing AI technology to generate instant, well written & personalized responses, at every guest touchpoint.

Fluent generated responses: all answers are in fluent natural language, essentially emulating human interaction
Upload PDF files: guestbooks, directions or suggestions and instantly train your assistant
Learn from previous conversations: upon connecting, PrimeHost will scan your previous conversations and use them to train your assistant
Customize everything: you can revise all imported information and manually edit or add anything you wish to share with your guests

Process

Four steps to start getting automated messages

SIGN UP

Easily create an account & choose your preferred program. We offer 1 month of free trial!

GRANT ACCESS

From your Booking.com and Airbnb account, grant access to PrimeHost for Inbox messages only.

BUILD PROPERTY

Add your property into PrimeHost’s database. Information will be extracted automatically and you can edit the content anytime.

SUBMIT

You will get notified through e-mail with a suggested response from PrimeHost, each time a guest contacts. You can approve the response instantly!
